Mô Tả Công Việc

AEONMALL Vietnam Co., Ltd is a core enterprise of AEON group, specializing in shopping mall development and business operation. Based on the basic philosophy “The customer first”, we are continuing our efforts to create malls that enhance the quality of life, stimulate local economic activity and contribute to community life and culture.
Because of business demand, we are in need of recruiting 01 Sales Officer (Leasing).

+ Work location: AEON MALL Hue – Vietinbank Building, No. 2 Le Quy Don Street, Phu Hoi Ward, Hue City, Thua Thien Hue (Temporary office).
+ Working time: On average 5 days/ week and in shift (A shift 8:30 AM – 5:30 PM & C shift 1:30 PM – 10:30 PM, about 5-7 C shifts per month), 1-2 OFF days/week not fixed on weekends, some shifts on public holidays.
+ Report to: Executive/ Senior Executive.

1. Approach potential customers/ tenants to offer space leasing for appropriate purposes (common areas in the mall like event spaces, meeting room/ hall, slots for wagons, LED screens for advertising, etc.) (20%);
2. Make action plan and budget plan for other income leasing activities (15%);
3. Process documentations (contract, payment, liquidation) to ensure smooth cooperation with customers/ tenants (15%);
4. Give necessary guidelines to customers/ tenants, supervise and evaluate effectiveness of space leasing activities, then timely share with customers/ tenants for any necessary adjustment or improvement (15%);
5. Prepare introduction materials, leasing kits, and guidelines for all other income areas in the mall (10%);
6. Support customers/ tenants to handle arising matters during space leasing time (10%);
7. Apply data tracking to make reports after each activity as required by the company or supervisor/ managers (10%);
8. Other tasks as assigned by supervisor or higher managers (5%).
